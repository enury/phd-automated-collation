Unique Errors in Manuscript N and Pithoeus' Edition

The following tables are discussed in Section 8.5.2.1: Editio Princeps vs. Manuscripts.

Witnesses present in the collation:

  • B Codex Monacensis Latinus 309, 15th century.
  • C Codex Chigianus Latinus H VIII 8, 15th century.
  • LH Håkanson's edition, 1978.
  • M Codex Monacensis Latinus 316, 16th century.
  • N Codex Bernensis (A) 9, 16th century.
  • P1594 Pithoeus editio princeps, 1594 (reprinted).

Unique Error in N1 and N2

There is one unique reading shared by N1 and N2, which could have been easily corrected: at ego dico meus es (N) versus est in other witnesses ('But I say, he is / you are mine').
